ü¶Ö The Thirsty Crow

Once, there was a thirsty crow. He flew here and there in search of water. But he could not find water anywhere. At last, he saw a pitcher in a garden. There was very little water in the pitcher. His beak could not reach the water. He saw some pebbles nearby. He thought of a plan. He picked up the pebbles one by one and dropped them into the pitcher. The water level rose up. The crow drank the water and flew away happily.

Moral: Where there is a will, there is a way.
