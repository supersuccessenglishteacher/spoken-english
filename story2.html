A lion was sleeping in a forest. A little mouse started playing on him. The lion woke up and caught the mouse. He wanted to kill it. The mouse said, "Please let me go, I will help you one day." The lion laughed and let him go. After some days, the lion was caught in a hunter's net. He roared for help. The little mouse came and bit the net with his sharp teeth. The lion became free. He thanked the mouse.

Moral: Kindness is never wasted.
